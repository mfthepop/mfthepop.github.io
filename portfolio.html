Where it all started

The photo shows me playing M.U.G.E.N. — Marvel vs. Capcom — at a summer. Inspired by this new interactive medium, I made it my mission to start creating games. I began with Macromedia Flash 5, and later moved on to Game Maker 5, as a form of self-driven learning.
With the help of dial-up internet and sprite databases, I worked on several prototypes — though they weren’t all that fun to play!
Eventually, I created my first game, Arata, which I hope to refine and upload to Steam someday!
